Newsletters & Publications

Patent Filed

31thJanuary, 2023

A Patent is filed in the Indian Patent Office on Thirty First January, Two Thousand Twenty-Three. The patent is titled 'Smart Energy Management And Control System'.The Patent Application Number is '202331006354'. The Patent comprises the Propriety ARMC Network Architecture used for Smart Energy Management And Control Systems. This is used for automation and monitoring Energy Consumption for both Residential and Industrial Uses. This Patent is filed under the DST TIDES NIDHI Prayas Scheme. The Patent is Published on Eighth September, Two Thousand Twenty-Three.

Project Completion Review at TIDES IIT Roorkee

13thDecember, 2022

On the Thirteenth December, Two Thousand Twenty-Two, the project completion review at TIDES IIT Roorkee was held.The panel of reviewers assessed the project. A live demonstration of working prototype was conducted. The reviewers found all the objectives completed. This marked the completion of the project. This doesn't end our story as the prototype need to be converted into a marketable product and potential investors required.

Progress Review Meeting at IIT Roorkee

27thJuly, 2022

On the Twenty Seventh July, Two Thousand Twenty-Two, progress review meeting was held at TIDES Business Incubation Office, IIT Roorkee.The panel of experts reviewed the progress and provided their insights. The committee was satisfied with the progress and hence decided to disperse rest of the funding amount.

Incubated By TIDES IIT Roorkee

09thJune, 2021

On Ninth June, Two Thousand Twenty-One, Suavy Technologies received the First Tranche from TIDES.Suavy Technologies Private Limited was incubated by TIDES IIT Roorkee. Suavy Technologies received funding of Rupees 10 Lakhs under the scheme of DST NIDHI Prayas. The first Tranche received, the equipment and materials required will be procured. The R&D work begins.

Suavy Technologies Founded

04thFeburary, 2021

On Fourth February, Two Thousand Twenty-One, Suavy Technologies Private Limited was Founded.Suavy Technologies Private Limited was founded by a bunch of B.Tech students in Electrical & Electronics Engineering with the dream of making India technologically independent. Alakshendra Singh, & Aniket Sourav were the Founding Directors with Dr. Aftab Alam as their mentor.

Suavy Technologies Selected for NIDHI Prayas

24thNovember, 2020

On Twenty Fourth November, Two Thousand Twenty, Suavy Technologies Private Limited was selected for NIDHI Prayas.Suavy Technologies Private Limited pitched their idea 'IoT Enabled Smart Energy Management System' to TIDES IIT Roorkee via an online medium. TIDES reviewed our idea and Selected us for their TIDES DST NIDHI Prayas 2020 program. TIDES allocated funds for 10 Lakhs Rupees and will be released as per the given milestones by Suavy Technologies.
